# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

- **Primary (B2C):** local residents who need a garment altered or repaired (hems, zippers, resizing, quick fixes). They arrive from local search, Google Maps, word of mouth, or a social bio link — usually on mobile — and want a fast answer to "does she do my fix, can I trust her, where is she?"
- **Secondary (B2B):** business decision-makers looking for a uniform supplier/repairer (larger tickets, recurring work).

## Product Purpose

Single-page landing for **Valdete Costuras**, a local sewing atelier. Its one job is converting visitors into **WhatsApp conversations** (sticky CTA + multiple in-page CTAs; a dedicated "orçamento para empresas" CTA for uniforms). No forms. Success = a WhatsApp tap.

## Brand Personality

**Costura Artesanal** — warm, handcrafted, trustworthy. Affectionate rather than clinical; premium-minimal bones (whitespace, restraint, high contrast) softened by artisanal warmth. Brand signatures: the dotted-circle "V" monogram, the Alex Brush script wordmark, and the *traço-costura* (stitch dashes) as the recurring motif. The Vermelho Valdete red is the only color voice.

## Anti-references

- Stock-photo sewing clichés (thimbles, measuring-tape borders, mannequin close-ups).
- Generic local-business template look; heavy gradients; multiple competing accent colors.
- Corporate/clinical coldness — this is a neighborhood atelier, not a SaaS.
- Fake social proof. There are no testimonials/photos yet; trust is built through clarity, the 4 differentiator pillars, and local presence — never invented reviews.
- Visible placeholders (`[WHATSAPP]`, `[ENDERECO]`, `[HORARIO]`, `[ANOS]`) shipping to end users.

## Design Principles

1. **Every road leads to WhatsApp.** One conversion action, distributed (hero, sections, sticky/floating, final band); zero friction, zero forms.
2. **Trust through clarity, not social proof.** Service scope, process, pillars, and location answer objections while photos/testimonials don't exist yet.
3. **The red thread is the voice.** One accent (Vermelho Valdete), used for CTAs and stitch details; coral/rosé are decorative only, never text.
4. **Motion is costura.** Every animation reads as sewing (running stitches, knots, seams), stays subtle, respects `prefers-reduced-motion`, and never risks Core Web Vitals or blank content without JS.
5. **Degrade gracefully.** Gallery/testimonials must look intentional while empty; the design never depends on assets that don't exist yet.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: contrast verified per token (accent-as-text uses `--accent-strong`), keyboard navigable with visible on-brand focus, semantic HTML with a single H1, tap targets ≥44px, `prefers-reduced-motion` honored everywhere, no horizontal overflow down to 320px.
