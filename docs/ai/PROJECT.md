- **Secondary (B2B):** uniforms for companies (uniformes/empresas) — larger tickets, recurring.

Fast, personal, fairly-priced sewing done by an experienced seamstress. Alterations for individuals; uniform production/repair for businesses.

## Primary Conversion

## Primary User Journey

### User
Local resident who needs a garment altered or repaired and wants it done quickly and reliably.

### Need
A trustworthy, nearby seamstress for a specific fix, with a clear sense of speed and fair price.

### Entry source
Local search, word of mouth, Google Maps, social media bio link.

### Expected path
Hero (understand what/where) → services (confirm the fix exists) → 4 trust pillars + how-it-works (reduce doubt) → WhatsApp CTA.

### Main objections
- "Do they do my specific repair?" → Services grid.
- "Can I trust them without reviews yet?" → 4 pillars, experience, local address.
- "How long / how much?" → How-it-works + fair-price pillar.
- "Where are they?" → Location section.

### Conversion action
Tap WhatsApp CTA (sticky + hero + section + final band).

### Abandonment risks
Unclear service scope, no visible location, slow load, unresolved placeholders showing to end users.

## Secondary Journey — Companies (uniforms)
