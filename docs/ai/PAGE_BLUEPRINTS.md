## Home Blueprint

### Objective
Convert local visitors into WhatsApp contacts; secondary capture of company uniform leads.

### Hierarchy
1. Hero headline + WhatsApp CTA (primary).
2. Services (primary offer proof).
3. Trust pillars + how-it-works (doubt reduction).
4. Companies/uniforms (secondary).
5. Location (local trust).
6. Final CTA.

### Responsive behavior
- Mobile-first. Header collapses to wordmark + WhatsApp icon button; floating WhatsApp button appears after hero.
- Services: 1 col (mobile) → 2 (tablet) → 3 (desktop).
- Pillars: 2×2 (mobile) → 1×4 (desktop).
- How-it-works: vertical steps (mobile) → horizontal (desktop).
- Gallery: 2 col (mobile) → 4 col (desktop).
- No horizontal overflow; generous section padding.

## Main Conversion Page Blueprint
Same as Home (single-page site). Conversion is WhatsApp; no separate lead page. Ensure every major section has a reachable CTA within one scroll.

## Services / Products Blueprint
Services rendered as a data-driven grid (array of `{icon, title, short}`) so items are easy to edit. Uniforms handled as a distinct block, not mixed into the alterations grid, to keep the two audiences legible.
