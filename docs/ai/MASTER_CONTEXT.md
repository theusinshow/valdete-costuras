## Project Summary
Single-page landing for **Valdete Costuras**, a local sewing atelier. Goal: convert visitors into WhatsApp conversations. Primary audience: alterations/repairs (local B2C). Secondary: uniforms for companies (B2B).

## Client and Business
Experienced seamstress offering alterations, repairs, and company uniforms. Neighborhood/city-local service, in-person. Logo not finalized; brand shown as typographic wordmark "Valdete Costuras".

## Primary Goal
WhatsApp contact (`[WHATSAPP]`). Sticky CTA + multiple in-page CTAs. Secondary goal: uniform quote requests from companies.

## User Journey Summary
Hero → alteration services → 4 trust pillars → how it works → uniforms/companies → gallery (placeholder) → testimonials (placeholder) → location & hours → final CTA → footer.

## Approved Architecture
11 sections, single page. See `SITE_ARCHITECTURE.md` and `PAGE_BLUEPRINTS.md`. Trust is built through clarity, the 4 differentiator pillars, and local presence — NOT through social proof (none available yet).

## Visual Direction
Direction **C — Costura Moderna**: minimal premium, high contrast, red-thread accent.
- Colors: bg `#FAFAF8`, text `#141414`, accent `#D8332A`, muted gray `#6B6B6B`.
- Type: Space Grotesk (display) + IBM Plex Sans (body), via Google Fonts.
- Layout: large typographic hero, edge-to-edge sections, fine details, generous whitespace.
Full tokens in `DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`.

## Motion Direction
Subtle and coherent with minimal-premium: short scroll reveals (fade/translate), sticky WhatsApp CTA, card hovers, accent transitions. No heavy libraries. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. See `MOTION_DIRECTION.md`.

## Technical Direction
**Next.js (App Router) + Tailwind CSS**. Static-friendly single page composed of section components. Slight overkill for one page; chosen for SEO, image handling, and future growth. `next/font` for Google Fonts. Deploy target open (Vercel recommended later).

## Quality Requirements
Semantic HTML, single coherent H1, WCAG AA contrast (verify accent `#D8332A` on light bg for text vs. UI use), keyboard nav, visible focus, optimized images/fonts, good LCP/CLS/INP, SEO title/description, local business relevance. See `QA_CHECKLIST.md`.

## Pending Content
WhatsApp number, address, hours, exact years of experience, real photos (before/after), testimonials, final logo, slogan. See `CONTENT_PENDING.md`.

## Open Risks
- Placeholders (`[WHATSAPP]`, `[ENDERECO]`, `[HORARIO]`, `[ANOS]`) must not ship visible to end users.
